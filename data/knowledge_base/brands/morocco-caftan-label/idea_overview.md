# Morocco Caftan Label

## Project Summary

A caftan or modest fashion label positioned on craftsmanship, occasion wear, cultural elegance, and made-to-order or curated collections.

## Why It Fits Morocco

- Textile and traditional clothing remain strategically important in Morocco.
- Occasion wear benefits from visual-led marketing and trust in finishing quality.
- Bridal, Eid, and event demand create natural seasonal campaigns.

## Primary Audiences

- brides and wedding families
- event buyers
- diaspora shoppers
- premium fashion buyers

## Core Offer

- made-to-order caftans
- capsule collections
- styling support
- seasonal event drops

## Messaging Angles

- elegant craftsmanship
- celebration-ready pieces
- heritage with contemporary styling
- custom fit and finishing confidence

## Proof And Trust Signals

- fabric closeups
- tailoring process
- delivery timelines
- client fittings
- event photography

## Tone

Elegant, precise, and aspirational. Avoid vague luxury clichés.
