# Morocco Leather Goods

## Project Summary

A leather accessories brand selling bags, wallets, belts, or travel pieces with a focus on craft, durability, and giftability.

## Why It Fits Morocco

- Leather goods fit Morocco's broader handicraft and heritage retail story.
- Buyers respond to material quality, finishing, and gift presentation.
- Tourism and export channels can both matter.

## Primary Audiences

- gift buyers
- style-conscious local buyers
- tourists
- export and boutique stockists

## Core Offer

- core accessories collection
- seasonal color drops
- gift sets
- custom or small-batch options

## Messaging Angles

- practical pieces with strong design character
- handcrafted finish
- long-lasting daily essentials
- easy gifting

## Proof And Trust Signals

- leather type
- stitching and finish detail
- packaging
- care guidance
- maker story

## Tone

Grounded, premium, and tactile. Avoid exaggerated heritage language that says nothing concrete.
