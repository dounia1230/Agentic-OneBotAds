# Morocco Surf Camp

## Project Summary

A surf camp or surf-and-stay brand selling beginner and intermediate surf experiences with accommodation, coaching, and community.

## Why It Fits Morocco

- Morocco's tourism tailwinds support destination-based experiences.
- Coastal surf destinations can combine sport, lifestyle, and travel.
- Strong visual storytelling works well for this category.

## Primary Audiences

- European surf travelers
- beginner surfers
- remote workers
- wellness and retreat buyers

## Core Offer

- accommodation plus surf lessons
- board rental
- airport pickup
- retreat or week-long packages

## Messaging Angles

- friendly beginner entry point
- surf plus stay convenience
- warm local atmosphere
- lifestyle and community, not only lessons

## Proof And Trust Signals

- lesson structure
- coach credentials
- beach access
- room visuals
- guest stories

## Tone

Relaxed, upbeat, and credible. Avoid generic "dream lifestyle" filler.
