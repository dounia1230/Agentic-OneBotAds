# Morocco SME Accounting SaaS

## Project Summary

A lightweight accounting, invoicing, or business operations SaaS for Moroccan SMEs that need simpler workflows and clearer financial tracking.

## Why It Fits Morocco

- Digital Morocco 2030 supports the growth of digital services, startups, and digital export capacity.
- SMEs need software that feels practical, local, and easy to adopt.
- Trust, onboarding, and support matter more than feature overload.

## Primary Audiences

- small business owners
- agencies
- retailers
- service businesses

## Core Offer

- invoicing
- expense tracking
- simple dashboards
- team access and support

## Messaging Angles

- easier daily admin
- less manual tracking
- built for busy small teams
- practical software with real support

## Proof And Trust Signals

- product demos
- onboarding clarity
- local support
- feature screenshots
- customer use cases

## Tone

Direct, useful, and anti-jargon. Avoid generic "digital transformation" slogans.
