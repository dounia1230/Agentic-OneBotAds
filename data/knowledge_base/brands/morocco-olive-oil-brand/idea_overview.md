# Morocco Olive Oil Brand

## Project Summary

A premium olive oil brand positioned on origin, taste, gifting, and kitchen trust for local and export buyers.

## Why It Fits Morocco

- Agriculture remains a strategic sector under Generation Green.
- Food brands can sell trust, origin, and gifting with strong visuals and education.
- Premium pantry products benefit from repeat purchase logic.

## Primary Audiences

- health-conscious households
- premium grocery buyers
- gift buyers
- restaurants and specialty stores

## Core Offer

- everyday bottle
- premium reserve bottle
- tasting set
- corporate or festive gift box

## Messaging Angles

- quality from origin and process
- a practical premium for everyday cooking
- beautiful giftable pantry product
- taste plus trust, not just generic wellness language

## Proof And Trust Signals

- harvest and sourcing notes
- packaging detail
- serving suggestions
- certifications when real
- retailer or chef endorsements

## Tone

Clean, culinary, and trustworthy. Avoid unsupported health claims.
