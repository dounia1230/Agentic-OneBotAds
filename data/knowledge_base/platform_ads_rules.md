# Platform Ads Rules

General advertising rules:
- Avoid misleading claims.
- Avoid guaranteed outcomes.
- Avoid fake urgency.
- Avoid unsupported performance promises.
- Avoid discriminatory targeting language.
- Use clear calls to action.
- Keep claims aligned with actual product capabilities.

LinkedIn:
- Use professional and business-focused messaging.
- Avoid overly casual or spammy language.
- Focus on business value, productivity, and decision-making.

Instagram:
- Use short, visual, energetic copy.
- Keep the caption clear and easy to scan.

Facebook:
- Use friendly and direct messaging.
- Avoid personal attribute assumptions.

Google Ads:
- Keep copy concise.
- Focus on the main user benefit.
- Avoid unsupported superlatives.
