# Morocco Language Academy

## Project Summary

A language school or tutoring project focused on French, English, Arabic, or business language outcomes for students and professionals.

## Why It Fits Morocco

- Education and employability offers align with Morocco's broader digital and service-economy push.
- Buyers care about outcomes, schedule fit, and teacher trust.
- Trials and clear level-based offers work well.

## Primary Audiences

- students
- job seekers
- professionals
- parents buying for children

## Core Offer

- group classes
- one-to-one sessions
- exam prep
- business communication programs

## Messaging Angles

- practical progress
- flexible learning
- teacher support
- outcomes tied to school, work, or mobility

## Proof And Trust Signals

- placement test
- curriculum clarity
- teacher qualifications
- student reviews
- class formats

## Tone

Encouraging and clear. Avoid promising instant fluency.
