# Morocco Market Signals

## Purpose

This file gives broad market context for starter project ideas aimed at Morocco.

Use it as background context, not as a substitute for company-specific facts.

## Tourism

Morocco is a strong travel market for hospitality, tours, experiences, and travel-linked retail.

As of January 9, 2025, Maroc.ma reported:
- 17.4 million visitors in 2024
- up 20% versus 2023
- 35% above 2019 levels

The Ministry of Tourism also reported:
- 112 billion dirhams in tourism revenue in 2024
- 28.7 million overnight stays in 2024

Marketing implication:
- travel, accommodation, guided experiences, transport add-ons, and giftable products can anchor campaigns on authenticity, convenience, and trusted local service.

## Handicrafts And Heritage Retail

As of February 4, 2025, Maroc.ma reported that handicraft exports exceeded MAD 1.11 billion in 2024, with pottery leading exports, followed by carpets and traditional clothing.

Marketing implication:
- handmade goods, caftans, pottery, leather goods, and decor can lean on design, heritage, giftability, and export readiness.

## Textile And Fashion

The Ministry of Industry states that the textile sector contributes 27% of industrial jobs and 7% of industrial added value.

Marketing implication:
- apparel, modest fashion, uniforms, and export-oriented fashion brands can be positioned on craftsmanship, speed, design, and production know-how.

## Automotive

The Ministry of Industry treats automotive as a flagship industrial sector and documents ecosystem support, skills training, industrial zones, and investment incentives.

Marketing implication:
- B2B offers in parts, sourcing, logistics, maintenance, and industrial services can use credibility, compliance, and reliability as the main message pillars.

## Agriculture And Food

Morocco's Generation Green 2020-2030 strategy positions agriculture as a major growth and resilience sector with emphasis on value creation, exports, jobs, and climate-smart production.

Marketing implication:
- olive oil, dates, premium food gifting, agri services, irrigation support, and farm-adjacent brands should balance quality cues with trust, origin, and water-aware responsibility.

## Digital And Startup Services

As of September 25, 2024, the Ministry in charge of digital transition published Digital Morocco 2030 and highlighted digital talents, digital startups, and outsourcing plus digital export.

The national cybersecurity strategy for 2030 also frames trusted digital infrastructure as a national priority.

Marketing implication:
- SaaS, agency, outsourcing, cybersecurity, and AI-enabled business tools should be positioned on productivity, reliability, compliance, and support.

## Renewable Energy

As of June 24, 2025, Maroc.ma reported that the Morocco Offer for green hydrogen is expected to accelerate investment in renewable energy, desalination, and downstream green industry.

Marketing implication:
- solar installers, energy audits, industrial efficiency, and sustainability services can frame value around savings, resilience, and long-term operating stability.
