# Morocco Argan Skincare

## Project Summary

A skincare or haircare project built around argan-origin storytelling, ingredient trust, and premium daily-use positioning.

## Why It Fits Morocco

- Morocco has strong global recognition around argan-linked products.
- Beauty buyers respond to origin, ritual, texture, and trust.
- Export and gifting angles can be strong when the brand is disciplined.

## Primary Audiences

- women buying self-care products
- gift buyers
- diaspora buyers
- export and boutique retail buyers

## Core Offer

- argan oil hero product
- skincare bundles
- haircare routines
- gift sets

## Messaging Angles

- origin and ingredient credibility
- simple routine with visible sensory appeal
- premium Moroccan beauty ritual
- gifting and export-ready presentation

## Proof And Trust Signals

- ingredients
- packaging quality
- usage guidance
- sourcing story
- before and after only when supportable

## Tone

Refined, sensory, and trustworthy. Avoid medical or unsupported treatment claims.
