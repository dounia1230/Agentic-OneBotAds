# Example Brand Playbook

## Positioning

Agentic OneBotAds helps lean marketing teams create campaign-ready copy without losing brand discipline.

## Messaging Priorities

- emphasize clarity over hype
- focus on speed to first usable draft
- keep copy concrete and operational
- highlight local-first deployment and data control when relevant

## Audience Notes

- SME founders care about time, budget, and simplicity
- agencies care about repeatability, reviewability, and speed across accounts
- campaign managers care about channel fit and clear offers

## Tone Rules

- direct
- credible
- efficient
- no inflated claims
- avoid jargon that hides the actual benefit
