# Morocco Cybersecurity Services

## Project Summary

A cybersecurity consultancy or managed service focused on SME protection, compliance readiness, and operational trust.

## Why It Fits Morocco

- Morocco's national cybersecurity strategy for 2030 reinforces the importance of trusted digital infrastructure.
- As more firms digitize, security moves from optional to necessary.
- Buyers need confidence, plain language, and risk reduction.

## Primary Audiences

- SMEs
- regulated service businesses
- digital agencies
- growing startups

## Core Offer

- security audit
- awareness training
- hardening and monitoring
- incident response support

## Messaging Angles

- reduce avoidable risk
- protect business continuity
- practical security for non-expert teams
- trusted local support

## Proof And Trust Signals

- audit framework
- response process
- certifications when real
- training format
- clear scope of work

## Tone

Serious and reassuring. Avoid fear-mongering and vague technical buzzwords.
