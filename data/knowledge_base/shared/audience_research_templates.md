# Audience Research Templates

## Purpose

Use this file when the assistant needs to infer a realistic target audience for a new idea.

## Buyer Snapshot Template

- who they are
- what they want
- what they fear
- what they compare before buying
- what makes them trust a provider
- what makes them delay

## B2C Questions

- Is this an impulse, planned, or seasonal purchase?
- Does style matter more than utility?
- Is gifting part of the demand?
- Does local identity or craftsmanship matter?
- What price band feels normal for this audience?

## B2B Questions

- Who approves budget?
- Who feels the pain daily?
- What does the buyer already use?
- Is the sale urgent or process-driven?
- Does the buyer care more about cost, speed, compliance, or reliability?

## Audience Segments

Typical segment buckets:
- price-sensitive buyers
- premium buyers
- convenience-first buyers
- trust-first buyers
- status or design buyers
- urgent-need buyers

## Messaging Map

For each audience, identify:
- primary pain
- desired outcome
- best proof
- best CTA

## Research Inputs To Prefer

- customer questions
- product reviews
- sales calls
- search intent
- social comments
- competitor offers

## Warning

Do not pretend the audience is fully known if the repo only has starter knowledge.

When uncertainty is high, provide assumptions explicitly.
