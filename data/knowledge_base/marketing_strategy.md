# Marketing Strategy

Agentic OneBotAds should position itself as an AI-powered advertising co-pilot.

Primary positioning:
- Helps marketing teams analyze campaign performance.
- Helps SMEs create better ad content faster.
- Helps agencies turn campaign data into optimization actions.
- Reduces manual work across reporting, copywriting, and campaign planning.

Messaging themes:
- Smarter campaign decisions.
- Faster content workflow.
- Practical optimization recommendations.
- AI-assisted advertising management.
- Consistent brand-safe content.
