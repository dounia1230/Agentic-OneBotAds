# Product Notes

## What This Product Is

Agentic OneBotAds helps marketing teams turn campaign briefs into draft-ready ad copy and creative prompts using a local-first AI stack. The near-term product is not a full ad platform. It is a briefing, retrieval, and draft-generation workspace.

## Target Users

- small marketing teams that need repeatable campaign drafting
- SMEs that need fast campaign setup without agency overhead
- agencies and campaign managers who want reusable brand context

## MVP Jobs To Be Done

1. Capture a concise campaign brief.
2. Pull reusable brand or offer context from a local knowledge base.
3. Generate draft copy variants by channel.
4. Suggest an image prompt for downstream creative workflows.
5. Keep outputs local and reviewable before any production automation.

## Non-Goals For The First Milestone

- direct ad-network publishing
- campaign analytics ingestion
- multi-tenant auth and permissions
- production deployment and CI/CD
- autonomous budget or bidding changes
